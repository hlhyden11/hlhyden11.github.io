Heather Hyden Map Portfolio

Fall and Spring 2016

Lexington Buildings By Age

This map shows the age of buildings in Downtown and on the Northside of Lexington. Darker hues represent older buildings and darker blues reflect the most recent construction. Additionally, the pale color represents missing PVA building age data.

The map was created in ArcGIS and CartoDB.

  • See the map:Lexington Buildings by Age
  • Data Source:Open Data Lexington
